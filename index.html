About Us

BBL Lights manufactures and distributes LED products such as LED Bulbs, LED Battens, LED Lights, LED Smart Lights, and so on. These were created under the supervision of an expert team. Our items have been evaluated using cutting-edge testing equipment, and the quality of our products has been monitored by engineers in our laboratory. Since then, we have been dedicated to manufacturing, supplying, wholesaling, and trading a vast array of high-quality LED Bulbs & Battens, LED Raw Materials, LED making tool kit, OEM LED, Philips & Syska Type LED Bulb and Philips & Syska Type LED Raw Material, Panel Light, LED Street Light, LED Flood Light, Led COB Light, and so on. We manufacture LED bulbs in a variety of wattages while complying quality controls in accordance with industry standards.To achieve perfect quality standards, we obtain raw materials from reputable and trustworthy vendors. We make and provide LED bulbs ranging in power from 3W to 80W in a variety of colours such as blue, green, orange, yellow, and white. They last a long time and can be used for both indoor and outdoor lighting.
